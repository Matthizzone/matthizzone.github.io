Bio

In May 2022, Matthew graduated with a Master's of Engineering in Computer Science from Cornell Tech, a campus of Cornell located on Roosevelt Island, NY. One year prior to that, he earned his Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at the Univeristy of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, which he completed in just three years. His coursework was nonspecific, ranging from advanced topics such a cyber security and machine learning, to niche topics like mixed reality research and interactive device design.

During his undergraduate education, Matthew was also considering a pathway through life sciences, completing coursework in organic chemistry and human anatomy, and the possibility of dentistry as a career but ultimately decided against it.

Before that, Matthew grew up on Long Island and attended Syosset Central School District, known for their caliber and high-performing graduates. There he laid the foundation for his academic efforts to come, while also participating in extracurricular activities such as research fairs, jazz bands, weekend bands, track and field, swimming, and even founded the Syosset High School Boy's Badminton team.

Matthew is both an analytical and creative thinker. His projects demostrate his ability to imagine creative solutions to design problems in many different areas. He is passionate about finding not just a solution, but the best solution. He enjoys working in small teams where he an effective communicator with not just colleagues but customers as well.

Matthew is currently looking for full-time employment. If you or your team are need of his skills, please refer to the contact information above.
