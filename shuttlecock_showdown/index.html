Shuttlecock Showdown

What's this game about?

Shuttlecock Showdown is a variation of a classic sports game: badminton... Except! On the other side of the court, there are enemies that are constantly shooting birdies at you. Instead of keeping up a rally, you use the racket to hit the raining birdies back to the enemies, which will take damage if you hit them. You also have a birdie gun for a stronger offense.
