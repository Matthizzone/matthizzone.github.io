Ace of Clubs

Summer of 2021

Ace of Clubs is a golfing video game built in Unity Game Engine. The objective is just like regular golf: sink the ball in the hole using as few strokes as possible; however, before each stroke you can play cards, which can either be powerups or attack cards. This title comes from a combination of card games and golf, hence, Ace of Clubs.

Shuttlecock Showdown

December 2021

Shuttlecock Showdown is a Virtual Reality game developed in Unity Game Engine. In this game, enemies are shooting badminton birdies at you while you defend yourself with your badminton racket and birdie launcher guns of your own. This was developed to satisfy final project requirements for a graduate course at Cornell Tech: Augmented and Virtual Reality.

Sight to Sound

February 2018

Sight to Sound is a concept for a wearable device as shown in the image here that takes in visual information from a camera (on the nosebridge), converts it into sound, and plays it out the earbuds. The conversion process involves the use of space-filling curves, a type of mathematical fractal. This project was the main focus of a mathematics research class I enrolled in high school.
