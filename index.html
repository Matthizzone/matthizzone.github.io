Shuttlecock Showdown

Nov 2021 - Dec 2021

A badminton-themed virtual reality shooter game developed in Unity for the Oculus Quest platform. The objective is to eliminate enemies as they are spawned onto the badminton court. The player is equipped with a racket and a birdie launcher gun. This project was a final project submissions for a Virtual and Augmented Reality, a graduate-level course at Cornell Tech.

Ace of Clubs

May 2021 - Aug 2021

A golfing video game developed in Unity for PC platform. The objective is just like real golf: sink the ball in as few strokes as possible. However, in this multiplayer game, players are dealt cards that can be played on others that either help or hinder their golfing game. The name comes from a play on words between golf and cards, hence Ace of Clubs.

Sight to Sound Project

Sep 2016 - May 2017

A hypothetical device was prototyped that could convert images to sound in a content-preserving way using space-filling curves, a kind of mathematical fractal. This has potential applications for enabling blind people to "see" with their ears. It was developed in Java on my laptop webcam and speakers, as was submitted to the Math Research Fair in my Junior year of high school.
