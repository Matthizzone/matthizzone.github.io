Sight to Sound

Sep 2016 - May 2017

What's this?

This device is a combination of glasses, a camera and earbuds (the camera is the small hole on the nose bridge). It takes live feed video footage, converts it into sound, and plays it into the earbuds.

Convert images to sound?

This "sensory substitution" was the key focus of this project. The main approach is explained in the video below.

Originally developed in Java in Eclipse IDE.

This project was presented at the 2017 Long Island Math Fair, where a gold medal was awarded. An academic paper was also written on the subject, which can be read here.
